#Area #ZenGarden

 Area home to the Marigold, a handful of PvZ Adventures plants, and Flower Pots and construction workers indoors and outdoors alike.

Plants without the #Amphibious, #Aquatic or #Aerial trait from other gardens, except [[Training Grounds]] and [[ZCorp Intra-Space-Time]], can spawn but are much less likely than plants native to this area.
