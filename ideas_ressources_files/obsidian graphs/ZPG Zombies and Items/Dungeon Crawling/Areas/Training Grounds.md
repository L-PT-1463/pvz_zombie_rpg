#Area #TrainingGrounds

Area where every player’s journey begins. It is mostly inspired by “I, Zombie” with its Cardboard Plants that are a perfect replica of the “I, Zombie” plant pool.

Upon returing to this area, you will also find a distinct sports theme amongst the items and spawnlings. The plant pool will also be extended to include all the non-cardboard plants native to the area, including the plant boss.
