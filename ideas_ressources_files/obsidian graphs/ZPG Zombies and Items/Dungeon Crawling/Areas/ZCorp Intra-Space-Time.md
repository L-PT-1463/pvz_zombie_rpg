#Area #ZCorpIntraSpaceTime

Area inspired by the Time Twister and ZCorp worlds of Penny's Pursuit from PvZ 2. It includes all the ZCorp zombies and trinkets and is mostly focused around the space aspect, taking a lot of inspiration from PvZ Heroes' environments. As its very concept is the rift in space-time, plants from all areas can spawn in ZCorp Intra-Space-Time.
